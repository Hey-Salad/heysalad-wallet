Frequently Asked Questions

What is HeySalad Wallet?
HeySalad Wallet is a non-custodial cryptocurrency wallet for TRON and other blockchains. You control your private keys and funds - we never have access to them.
I lost my recovery phrase. Can you help me recover it?
No. This is a non-custodial wallet, which means we never have access to your recovery phrase or private keys. If you lose your recovery phrase, your funds are permanently lost. This is why it's critical to write it down and store it securely.
How do I backup my wallet?
When you create a wallet, you'll receive a 12-word recovery phrase. Write this down on paper and store it in a safe place. Never share it with anyone. This phrase is the ONLY way to recover your wallet.
I sent crypto to the wrong address. Can you reverse it?
No. Blockchain transactions are irreversible by design. We cannot cancel, reverse, or refund transactions. Always double-check the recipient address before sending.
Why is my balance showing 0 TRX?
New wallet addresses start with 0 balance until you receive your first transaction. You'll need to receive TRX from another wallet or purchase it from an exchange.
What are network fees?
Network fees (also called gas fees) are paid to blockchain validators to process your transactions. These fees go to the blockchain network, not to HeySalad. Fees vary based on network congestion.
Is my data private?
Yes! We do NOT collect your personal data, location, or usage analytics. Your private keys are stored only on your device in encrypted form. See our Privacy Policy for details.
What blockchains do you support?
Currently, we support TRON (TRX). Support for Solana, Polkadot, and Avalanche is coming soon.
Can I use HeySalad Wallet on multiple devices?
Yes, but you'll need to import your wallet on each device using your 12-word recovery phrase. Each device stores the keys independently.
Is HeySalad Wallet safe?
Yes. Your private keys are stored in iOS Secure Enclave with hardware-backed encryption. We use biometric authentication (Face ID/Touch ID) for all sensitive operations. However, security ultimately depends on you keeping your recovery phrase safe.
What is testnet vs mainnet?
Testnet: A test blockchain with fake crypto for development and testing. No real value.
Mainnet: The real blockchain with real crypto that has real monetary value.
You can switch networks in the app settings.
Do you provide tax advice or reporting?
No. You are responsible for your own tax obligations. We recommend consulting with a tax professional regarding cryptocurrency taxes in your jurisdiction.
Can I get a refund?
HeySalad Wallet is free to download and use. We do not charge any fees, so there are no refunds. Network fees paid to blockchain validators are non-refundable.
Is HeySalad Wallet available on Android?
Currently, HeySalad Wallet is iOS-only. Android support may come in the future.
What is the minimum age to use HeySalad Wallet?
You must be 18 years or older to use HeySalad Wallet. The app is rated 17+ on the App Store.

Common Issues and Solutions

Transaction is Pending

Blockchain transactions can take a few seconds to a few minutes depending on network congestion. Check the transaction status on the blockchain explorer.

Transaction Failed

Common reasons:

  • Insufficient balance to cover network fees
  • Network congestion or temporary issues
  • Invalid recipient address

Note: Network fees are charged even for failed transactions.

App Won't Open

  • Make sure you have the latest version from the App Store
  • Restart your device
  • Check your internet connection
  • If issue persists, contact support
